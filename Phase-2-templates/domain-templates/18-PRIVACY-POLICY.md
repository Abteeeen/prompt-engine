## 🎯 TEMPLATE

```
ROLE: You are a data privacy attorney and compliance specialist with 15+ years experience in GDPR, CCPA, and global privacy regulations.
Your specialty: Creating clear, compliant privacy policies that build user trust while meeting legal requirements.

CONTEXT: I operate a [BUSINESS_TYPE: website/app/SaaS/e-commerce] that [DATA_ACTIVITIES: collects user data/processes payments/tracks behavior].
My users are primarily located in [USER_LOCATIONS: EU/US/global].
Data I collect includes: [DATA_TYPES: email/payment/location/usage].
Third-party services: [SERVICES: analytics/payment/email/hosting].

TASK: Create a comprehensive privacy policy and terms of service that comply with [PRIMARY_REGULATION: GDPR/CCPA/both] and build user trust.

REQUIREMENTS:
- PRIVACY POLICY:
  * What data we collect and why (specific purposes)
  * How we collect it (forms, cookies, third parties)
  * How we use it (processing purposes)
  * Who we share it with (third-party categories)
  * Data retention periods (specific timeframes)
  * User rights (access, deletion, portability, objection)
  * Security measures (encryption, access controls)
  * International transfers (if applicable)
  * Contact information for privacy inquiries
  * Last updated date

- TERMS OF SERVICE:
  * Acceptance of terms
  * Service description and limitations
  * User obligations and prohibited conduct
  * Account termination conditions
  * Intellectual property rights
  * Disclaimers and limitation of liability
  * Governing law and dispute resolution
  * Changes to terms procedure
  * Contact information

CONSTRAINTS:
- NO copy-paste from generic templates without customization
- NEVER claim compliance you can't prove (encryption, security)
- NO vague retention periods — be specific (e.g., "7 years for tax purposes")
- NEVER bury important information in dense legalese
- NO checkbox without clear explanation of what user agrees to

AUDIENCE: [USER_TYPE: consumers/businesses/children] with [LEGAL_SOPHISTICATION: high/medium/none] who need [TRUST_LEVEL: maximum/balanced]

OUTPUT FORMAT:
**PRIVACY POLICY**

Last Updated: [DATE]

1. INTRODUCTION
[Who we are, what this policy covers]

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
2.1 Information You Provide
[What users actively submit]

2.2 Information Automatically Collected
[Cookies, analytics, device info]

2.3 Information from Third Parties
[Payment processors, social login]

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
[Specific purposes with legal basis]

4. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION
[Categories of recipients]

5. YOUR RIGHTS
[GDPR/CCPA rights with how to exercise]

6. DATA RETENTION
[Specific timeframes by data type]

7. SECURITY
[Measures taken, not detailed enough to compromise security]

8. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
[If data leaves user's jurisdiction]

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
[COPPA/compliance statement]

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
[How users will be notified]

11. CONTACT US
[Privacy officer contact]

**TERMS OF SERVICE**
[Parallel structure for ToS]

SELF-CHECK BEFORE RESPONDING:
Rate your policies:
- Regulatory compliance (1-3):
- User comprehensibility (1-3):
- Business protection (1-3):
If total < 7 — add specificity to data handling and simplify language.
```

## 🎨 REAL EXAMPLES

**Example 1: Data Collection Section**
```
3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

3.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
• Email address (for login and communication)
• Name (for personalization)
• Password (encrypted, we cannot see it)
• Profile photo (optional)

3.2 Usage Information
We automatically collect:
• IP address and device type (for security and optimization)
• Pages visited and features used (to improve our service)
• Time spent on platform (usage analytics)
• Referral source (marketing attribution)

Legal Basis: GDPR Article 6(1)(b) — contract performance and legitimate interests
```

**Example 2: User Rights (GDPR)**
```
6. YOUR RIGHTS

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

• Access: Request a copy of your personal data
• Correction: Update inaccurate information
• Deletion: Request we delete your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
• Restriction: Limit how we process your data
• Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
• Objection: Opt out of certain processing (including marketing)

To exercise these rights, email privacy@[company].com with "Privacy Request" in the subject.
We respond within 30 days as required by law.
```

## ✅ QUALITY CHECKLIST

- [ ] Each data type has specific purpose listed
- [ ] Third parties are named by category (analytics, payment, etc.)
- [ ] Retention periods are specific (not "as long as necessary")
- [ ] User rights include HOW to exercise them (email, form, etc.)
- [ ] Legal basis for processing is stated (GDPR compliance)
- [ ] Cookie policy is separate or clearly marked section
- [ ] Children's section addresses COPPA if under-13 possible
- [ ] International transfer mechanism explained (SCCs, adequacy)
- [ ] Contact email is monitored (not black hole)
- [ ] Last updated date is within last 12 months

## 💡 PRO TIPS

- **Plain language first**: Write for a 12-year-old, then layer in legal precision
- **Specific beats vague**: "We use encryption" → "We use TLS 1.3 for transmission and AES-256 for storage"
- **Layer your policies**: Summary at top, details below, full legal text at bottom
- **Review third-party policies**: Ensure your descriptions match their actual practices
- **Update triggers**: Set calendar reminders for annual review plus after any new data collection
- **Trust signals**: Include security certifications, trust badges, and clear contact info

## 🔄 ITERATION WORKFLOW

1. **Data audit**: List every data element you collect and process
2. **Purpose mapping**: Document why you collect each data type
3. **Third-party inventory**: Catalog all services with data access
4. **Draft policy**: Write using template, customize for your business
5. **Legal review**: Have privacy attorney review for compliance
6. **User testing**: Ask non-lawyers to read and summarize key points
7. **Implementation**: Publish, link in footer, cookie banner integration
8. **Ongoing**: Review quarterly, update for new regulations/features
