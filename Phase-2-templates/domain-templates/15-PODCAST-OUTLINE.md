## 🎯 TEMPLATE

```
ROLE: You are a veteran podcast producer who has crafted episodes for chart-topping shows with millions of downloads.
Your specialty: Creating outlines that balance structure with spontaneity, keeping listeners engaged for the full episode.

CONTEXT: I'm recording a [PODCAST_FORMAT: interview/solo/panel] episode for my show about [PODCAST_TOPIC/NICHE].
Episode length target: [TARGET_LENGTH] minutes
Guest/Topic: [GUEST_NAME] or [EPISODE_SUBJECT]
Listener avatar: [TARGET_AUDIENCE] who wants [LISTENER_OUTCOME]

TASK: Create a detailed podcast episode outline with segments, talking points, transitions, and monetization CTAs.

REQUIREMENTS:
- COLD OPEN (0-60 sec): Hook that starts in the middle of the action or most compelling insight
- INTRO (60-120 sec): Show intro + episode tease + listener promise
- MAIN SEGMENTS (3-5): Each with specific talking points, questions, and timestamp targets
- TRANSITIONS: Verbatim bridge scripts between segments
- MOMENTS: 2-3 " quotable moments" designed for social media clips
- SPONSOR INTEGRATION: Natural product/service mentions (if applicable)
- OUTRO CTA: Review ask, subscription ask, next episode tease, website mention
- BACKGROUND: Suggested music/vibe notes for each segment

CONSTRAINTS:
- NO "Welcome to the show" generic openings
- NO "So, tell us about yourself" lazy interview questions
- NEVER read questions verbatim — reframe naturally
- NO more than 90 seconds of intro before value delivery
- Avoid yes/no questions — frame for stories and specifics

AUDIENCE: [DEMOGRAPHIC] with [INTEREST_LEVEL] interest in [TOPIC_AREA]

OUTPUT FORMAT:
**EPISODE TITLE OPTIONS** (3 options under 60 characters)

**COLD OPEN** (0-60 sec)
[Exact script or direction]

**SEGMENT 1: [NAME]** (timestamp target)
- Goal of segment: [What listener learns/feels]
- Talking points/questions (3-5):
  1. [Specific question/story prompt]
  2. [Follow-up for depth]
  3. [Challenge/insight angle]
- Transition to next: [Exact handoff script]

[Repeat for all segments]

**MOMENTS FOR CLIPS** (with timestamps)
1. [Quote-worthy moment description]
2. [Controversial/hot take moment]
3. [Actionable insight moment]

**OUTRO SEQUENCE** (2-3 minutes)
- Episode recap: [Key takeaways summary]

- CTA 2: [Website/resource mention]
- Next episode tease: [Hook for next week]

**SHOW NOTES** (100-word description with timestamps)

```

## 🎨 REAL EXAMPLES

**Example 1: Interview Episode**
```
COLD OPEN:
Start with guest's most counterintuitive take: 
"Most founders are building the wrong thing first. [pause] Here's why..."
[Interview clip plays — 30 sec of strongest moment]

SEGMENT 1: The Mistake Everyone Makes (0-5 min)
- Lead with specific failure story, not biography
- "Walk me through the week before you shut down [Company]. What were you seeing?"
- "What did you ignore that you wish you'd listened to?"
- Transition: "That failure led to your current success. [pause] But not immediately..."

CLIP MOMENT:
"The startups that die don't run out of money. They run out of belief. Money's just the symptom."
```

**Example 2: Solo Episode**
```
COLD OPEN:
"I made $50K last month from a side project I started in 3 hours. [pause] Here's the embarrassing truth: I almost didn't start because I thought it was too simple."

SEGMENT STRUCTURE:
1. The 3-hour origin story (vulnerability + surprise)
2. The "simple" framework (teachable moment)
3. Why complexity kills (insight)
4. How to find your simple thing (actionable)
5. The catch (honest constraint)
```

## 🔧 VARIATIONS

**Variation 1: News/Current Events**
```
Cold open: Immediate take on breaking news/developing story
Segments: What happened → Why it matters → What happens next → What you should do
Tone: Urgent but measured, expert analysis
Timing: Released within 24 hours of news breaking
```

**Variation 2: Q&A/Listener Episode**
```
Cold open: Aggregate listener question theme
Segments: Question 1 → Question 2 → Question 3 with connecting thread
Interaction: Play voice messages from listeners
CTA: Encourage more questions for future episodes
```

**Variation 3: Story/Narrative Episode**
```
Cold open: Start at climax, then " rewind"
Structure: Present moment → Flashback → Build up → Return to present → Lesson
Production: Music-heavy, sound design, possible voice actors
Length: Usually longer (45-60 min)
```

## ✅ QUALITY CHECKLIST

- [ ] Cold open starts with the most compelling moment, not setup
- [ ] First question/story gets to the core immediately
- [ ] Every segment has clear "what's in it for the listener"
- [ ] Transitions are scripted verbatim, not left to chance
- [ ] At least 3 quotable moments identified for social clips
- [ ] Sponsor mention feels organic, not forced
- [ ] Outro has distinct asks for review, subscribe, and website
- [ ] Show notes have timestamps for easy navigation

## 💡 PRO TIPS

- **The "Start at the end" technique**: Begin with the most dramatic moment, then circle back
- **Use the "Yes, and" interview style**: Build on their answer instead of jumping to next question
- **Silence is content**: Leave 2-3 second pauses after powerful statements
- **Prepare the guest**: Send 3-5 questions in advance, but be ready to go off-script
- **Record room tone**: 30 seconds of silence for editing gaps
- **The " one thing" rule**: Each segment should deliver ONE clear takeaway

## 🔄 ITERATION WORKFLOW

1. **Outline draft**: Structure segments and talking points
2. **Question polish**: Reframe generic questions to be specific
3. **Transition scripting**: Write exact handoff language
4. **Clip identification**: Mark 3 moments for social media
5. **Post-recording**: Note where energy dipped, adjust for next time
6. **Listener feedback**: Read reviews, adjust format based on requests
