2. What's the 20% effort that yields 80% results?
3. What am I avoiding that I need to face?

- Inbox to zero (or scheduled)
- Review completed tasks
- Write tomorrow's MITs
- Clear physical and digital workspace
- "Close" the workday mentally

```

```

**Variation 3: Team Lead/Manager**
```
Focus: Balance individual work with team support
Add: Office hours, 1:1 blocks, team meeting rhythms
Remove: Uninterrupted deep work assumptions
Emphasis: Async communication protocols, documentation systems
```

## 💡 PRO TIPS

- **Protect your mornings**: Most people have peak focus 2-4 hours after waking
- **The "maker's schedule"**: Creative work needs 4-hour blocks, not 1-hour slots
- **Time blocking is commitment**: When you schedule deep work, treat it like a meeting with your most important client — yourself
- **Energy management > time management**: Match task difficulty to energy levels
- **The 2-minute rule**: If something takes <2 min and can't be batched, do it now
- **Sunday prep**: 30 min on Sunday reviewing the week ahead prevents Monday chaos
- **Track adherence**: Note what's working and what's not — iterate monthly

## 🔄 ITERATION WORKFLOW

1. **Week 1-2**: Test the system, note friction points
2. **Week 3**: Adjust based on actual energy patterns and interruptions
3. **Week 4**: Lock in the refined system
4. **Monthly review**: What's working? What's not? Any life changes to accommodate?
