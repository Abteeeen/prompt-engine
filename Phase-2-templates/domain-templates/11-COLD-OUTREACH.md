**Best For**: Multi-touch cold email and LinkedIn sequences for B2B sales
**Effectiveness**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Highest for outbound sales)

## 🎯 TEMPLATE

```
ROLE: You are an expert B2B sales copywriter with 15+ years experience in cold outreach that converts.
Your specialty: Writing personalized, value-first sequences that get replies, not unsubscribes.

CONTEXT: I need to reach out to [TARGET_PROSPECT] at [TARGET_COMPANY] who are [PROSPECT_SITUATION/PROBLEM].
My solution is [YOUR_SOLUTION] which delivers [KEY_BENEFIT].

TASK: Create a 3-5 touch cold outreach sequence (email + LinkedIn) that builds trust and drives a [CALL_TO_ACTION].

REQUIREMENTS:
- Touch 1: Hyper-personalized opener referencing specific company/person insight (NOT "I noticed you're...")
- Touch 2: Value-first follow-up with relevant insight or resource (no "just checking in")
- Touch 3: Social proof case study from similar company/industry
- Touch 4: Direct ask with clear value proposition and urgency
- Touch 5: Breakup email that leaves door open
- Each touch must have specific subject line under 50 characters
- LinkedIn touches should reference mutual connections or recent posts if applicable
- Tone: Professional but conversational, never salesy or desperate

CONSTRAINTS:
- NEVER use "I" more than once per email
- NO mention of "quick call" or "15 minutes" before Touch 4
- NO generic benefits like "save time" or "increase revenue"
- NO attachments or calendar links in first 2 touches
- Maximum 125 words per touch

AUDIENCE: [DECISION_MAKER_ROLE] at [COMPANY_SIZE] companies in [INDUSTRY]

OUTPUT FORMAT: Deliver as numbered sequence with:
- Touch #
- Channel (Email/LinkedIn)
- Subject line
- Full message body
- Recommended send timing (Day 1, Day 3, etc.)

SELF-CHECK BEFORE RESPONDING:
Rate your sequence:
- Personalization depth (1-3): 
- Value-first approach (1-3):
- Clear progression (1-3):
If total < 7 — add more specificity before sending.
```

## 🔧 VARIATIONS

**Variation 1: Event-Based Sequence**
```
Trigger: Prospect attended webinar/downloaded content
Angle: Reference their engagement + immediate value
Timing: Day 0 (immediate), Day 2, Day 5, Day 10

```

Timing: 3-6 months after last contact

## ✅ QUALITY CHECKLIST

- [ ] First line is SPECIFIC to this prospect (not templated)
- [ ] Every touch provides VALUE before asking for anything
- [ ] Subject lines are under 50 characters and curiosity-driven
- [ ] Clear CTA only appears in Touches 4-5
- [ ] Social proof is from similar industry/company size
- [ ] Sequence has logical narrative arc (problem → insight → proof → ask)

---

## 💡 PRO TIPS

- **Use the "Pattern Interrupt" technique**: Start with something unexpected that breaks their autopilot scanning
- **Apply the "Give Give Give Ask" ratio**: Provide 3 pieces of value before any request
- **Use specificity as social proof**: "34% reduction" beats "significant improvement" every time
- **Write the subject line LAST**: It should capture the ONE thing they'd want to open for
- **Test breakup emails carefully**: They should feel genuine, not manipulative
- **Apply COSTAR framework**: Context → Objective → Style → Tone → Audience → Response format
